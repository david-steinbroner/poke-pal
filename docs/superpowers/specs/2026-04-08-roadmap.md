## Product Strategy (decided 2026-04-10)

### Two-page architecture
- **League pages** = "Here's what's good" — metas, recommended teams, copy strings. Aspirational and reference-oriented.
- **Teams page** = "Build with what you have" — discovery search string, coverage-based suggestions, type gap guidance. The personal workshop.

### The handoff
Players browse league metas and recommended teams. When they inevitably don't have the right Pokemon at the right CP, the Teams page becomes the coach — helping them find complementary Pokemon in their actual GO storage via discovery search strings, and guiding them by type coverage gaps.

### What Poke Pal is NOT (from competitive research)
- Not an encyclopedia (PvPoke/GO Hub own that)
- Not a battle simulator (Pokebattler owns that)
- Not a news site (Leek Duck/GO Hub own that)
- Not a multi-game platform
- Not a social network

### Rejected approaches
- **PvPoke rankings integration** — Deferred. Matchup data only has top 5 per Pokemon (fatal flaw). Curated teams are more "coach-like" and faster to ship. Revisit after user feedback.
- **Full Pokedex as goal** — Rejected. We curate, not catalog. 1560 Pokemon for search/counter is fine, but meta picks stay manually curated per league.
- **Raid tab** — Rejected. Raids stay as compact pills on home. Pokebattler owns raids deeply.
- **Hamburger menu** — Rejected. 3 tabs (Home, Leagues, Teams) is sufficient.
- **Type-coverage-only team rating** — Replaced. Initial version was tier-dominant (3 S-tiers = "S" rating even with terrible coverage). Rewritten to be coverage-dominant with hard shared-weakness penalties.

## Next Steps (prioritized)

### Teams Page Enhancement
1. **Coverage coaching** — when meta suggestions fall short, guide by type gaps. "You need Fire or Fighting coverage" with actionable search strings.
2. **Adaptive suggestions** — suggestions that change character once player picks non-meta Pokemon. Shift from "here are S-tiers" to "here's what covers your gaps."
3. **Discovery string prominence** — "Find Teammates in GO" is the most important feature for 80% of users. Needs better positioning and explanation on Teams page.

### League Page Polish
4. **Your Team container as nudge** — when team is incomplete, container should say "Go to Team Builder to complete" not just show the partial team.
5. **Curated team tapping** — verify the add-from-recommended-team flow works smoothly end-to-end.

### Pre-Launch
6. **GBL schedule automation** — stale data is the #1 launch blocker. Need at minimum date-gated filter at build time.
7. **Rachel design polish** — after functionality is stable, visual design pass.
8. **Reddit launch** — r/TheSilphArena, timed to GBL season/cup rotation.

### Post-Launch (based on user feedback)
9. **PvPoke rankings integration** — if users want algorithmic team rating, build the matchup-based system. Spec at `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-04-10-pvpoke-rankings-integration.md`.
10. **Discord copy format** — "Copy for Discord" button that outputs formatted team + search string + Poke Pal link.
11. **SEO long-tail** — programmatic pages for "pokemon go [cup name] search string" queries.
12. **Better counter scoring** — factor in STAB, bulk, energy generation (currently ATK-only).
